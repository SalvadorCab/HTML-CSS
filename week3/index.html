WHAT IS AGE OF EMPIRES 2?

Age of Empires 2 is a videogame released in 1999 by Microsoft. This is a strategy game where you can control an entire civilization from Middle Ages times, defeat enemies, forge alliances and transform your kingdom into a mighty empire. There are many ways to achieve victory, and the game allows up till 8 players simultaneously.

RULES

The rules during the game for each category are as it follows:

  • 1 vs 1
    • First Round: Both players are assigned civilizations randomly. Map is assigned randomly. The match should not last more than 2 hours. If there is no winner by that time, the player with more points wins the round.
    • Second Round: The loser of round 1 chooses map and the winner chooses civilization first. After the first civilization (winner) and map is set, player two chooses civilization. The match cannot last more than 2 hours, in which case the player with more points automatically wins.
    • Third Round: In case that the previous rounds were won by different players, round three must be played, otherwise the winner player automatically classifies to the next level of finals. For the third round, map is chosen by the loser of round 2 and the winner of round 2 chooses civilization. Then the loser of round 2 chooses civilization. Time rules apply the same. The winner of this round classifies immediately to the next final level.
  • 2 vs 2
    • First Round: both teams are assigned civilizations randomly. Map is assigned randomly. The match should not last more than 2 hours. If there is no winner by that time, the team ofthe player with more points wins the round.
    • Second Round: The loser team of round 1 chooses map and the winner team chooses civilizations first. After the first civilizations (winner team) and map is set, team two chooses civilization. The match cannot last more than 2 hours, in which case the team with the player with more points automatically wins.
    • Third Round: In case that the previous rounds were won by different teams, round three must be played, otherwise the winner team automatically classifies to the next level of finals. For the third round, map is chosen by the loser team of round 2 and the winner team of round 2 chooses civilizations. Then the loser team of round 2 chooses civilizations. Time rules apply the same. The winner team of this round classifies immediately to the next final level.
  • 3 vs 3
    • First Round: both teams are assigned civilizations randomly. Map is assigned randomly. The match should not last more than 2 hours. If there is no winner by that time, the team ofthe player with more points wins the round.
    • Second Round: The loser team of round 1 chooses map and the winner team chooses civilizations first. After the first civilizations (winner team) and map is set, team two chooses civilization. The match cannot last more than 2 hours, in which case the team with the player with more points automatically wins.
    • Third Round: In case that the previous rounds were won by different teams, round three must be played, otherwise the winner team automatically classifies to the next level of finals. For the third round, map is chosen by the loser team of round 2 and the winner team of round 2 chooses civilizations. Then the loser team of round 2 chooses civilizations. Time rules apply the same. The winner team of this round classifies immediately to the next final level.
